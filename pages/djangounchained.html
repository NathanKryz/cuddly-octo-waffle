Django Unchained (2012)



With the help of a German bounty-hunter, a freed slave sets out to rescue his wife from a brutal plantation owner in Mississippi.

Why I enjoy it:


On top of a rather visceral and down to earth portrayal of the struggles of a period in American History, the story of the resolve of the main character, Django, in his search is a work of art. And the chemistry between him and Dr. Schultz makes for one of the most entertaining on screen duos out there.
