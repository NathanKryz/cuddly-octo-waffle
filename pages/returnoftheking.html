Lord of the Rings: Return of the King (2003)



Gandalf and Aragorn lead the World of Men against Sauron's army to draw his gaze from Frodo and Sam as they approach Mount Doom with the One Ring.

Why I enjoy it:


A fantastic climatic conclusion to a long-standing trilogy. A great cumulation of all the character plots up to this point. Some of the best and most cinematic battles on the big screen.
