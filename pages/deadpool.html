Deadpool (2016)



A wisecracking mercenary gets experimented on and becomes immortal yet hideously scarred, and sets out to track down the man who ruined his looks.

Why I enjoy it:


A good blend of action and comedy, featuring good pacing of both plot and the jokes to keep things fresh, nothing overstays its welcome or drags out scenes. Overall just a solid and fun time.
