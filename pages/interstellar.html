Interstellar (2014)



When Earth becomes uninhabitable in the future, a farmer and ex-NASA pilot, Joseph Cooper, is tasked to pilot a spacecraft, along with a team of researchers, to find a new planet for humans.

Why I enjoy it:


Fantastic cinematography, stunning visuals, and a rather profound commentary that explores a possible what-if for the future and the struggles that would come with it. Also has a fantastic and memorable musical score.
