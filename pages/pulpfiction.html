Pulp Fiction (1994)



The lives of two mob hitmen, a boxer, a gangster and his wife, and a pair of diner bandits intertwine in four tales of violence and redemption.

Why I enjoy it:


A great tale of the more down-to-earth and modern day struggles of various individuals trying to make the better of their situations despite everything trying to prevent them doing otherwise. Big fan of the various story arcs the characters go through, and Sameul L. Jackson absolutely steals the show in some of the most memorable and quotable sequences out there.
